THE LEGACY
WHO WE ARE
The Khattar Group of Companies, founded in 1954, set up its first factory to manufacture brass components, catering to the electric and transport industries. Over the past 70 years, this group has diversified into various sectors, such as:

Vishnu Crucibles Pvt. Ltd.

Trades into crucibles, required for melting alloys.

Khattar Marine Services Pvt. Ltd.

Is into marine transportation, stevedoring, ship breaking and provisioning of tugs.

Saffron Enterprises Pvt. Ltd.

Is sole distributor of Madura Garments for Peter England across the state of Gujarat since 2000. This includes supplies to more than 100 outlets across Gujarat with multiple retail stores across the state.

Venus Developers

Is into real estate development.

Venus Brassotech

It extrudes high quality brass rods in various shapes and sizes to meet the demands of various industrial applications as well as the specific needs of architects and designers.

Venus Structural Consultants

Is into Structural Designing, Industrial Planning and Project Management Consultancy service. Through their vast experience and expertise, they have delivered solutions adhering to the needs which are compliant to statutory, local as well as international norms.
