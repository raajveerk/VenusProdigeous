Q&A
Will you undertake the project on a turnkey basis?

Yes. Venus Prodigious India is a full service, turnkey interior design firm. We offer complete interior design services with interior construction / fit-outs including civil construction, false ceiling, electrical, plumbing, water-proofing, fitted carpentry, loose furniture and painting + polishing. From conceptual design to handover, our team will be fully responsible for managing your project, co-ordinating with all vendors and sub-contractors, for maintaining timelines and ensuring quality control. We are always by your side until you are 100% satisfied.

How do you ensure that quality is maintained?

Venus Prodigious India is one of the best interior designers in Jamnagar and in India. For us, quality begins with thorough documentation and this includes our detailed estimates which specify brands and grades of materials that will be used in the construction ofyour home. Our trained site supervisors then ensure that materials are received and that construction is in strict accordance with the approved BOQ and drawing sets. Supplies are photographed and catalogued. Any discrepancies are immediately brought to the notice of the design team. In addition to these measures, the designer in charge of the project also visits the site every week to have an overview of the progress.

What brands do you use?

Regardless of budgets, it is our policy to only use recognized high quality branded materials. This includes plywood, block-boards, veneers and laminates, adhesives, all hardware, wires and switches, POP, gypsum boards as well as paints and polishes. This ensures not only a high quality finish but also durability and longevity of your residential interior design.

Will you work with external contractors?

Yes. We also work with external contractors. In such case/s we shall provide all the necessary drawing sets and carry out periodic site visits.

Will you work on labour basis?

No. Since we offer a premium service, all of our input costs are included within the quoted rates.

Can we select from our preferred vendors?

Absolutely. Though we are always happy to suggest good vendors as are required for the project, we are also most happy to select materials from the vendors of your choice.

My site is not in Jamnagar – can you still execute it for me?

Yes. However, we do not hire local contractors and agencies and will send our construction teams from Jamnagar. As one of the best interior designers in India, this is the only way in which we can guarantee that quality standards are maintained and that timelines are observed. In such case, outstation charges would be applicable in addition to our standard rates.

Do you offer support after the project is complete?

Absolutely. As a company, we believe in building lasting relations with our clients and raising the bar for interior designers in India across all aspects of design, construction and client servicing. We are always here to help you with any assistance that you may require, be it connecting you with a vendor, following up on a warranty or for periodic maintenance.
